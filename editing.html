Video Editing

Since 2017, I have been making videos for my youtube channel. I make these videos because I enjoy making them. Most of the videos on my channel do not have heavy editing, but there are some that I put a lot of passion and work into in order to make them good for me. I enjoy making videos because it lets me be as creative as I want to. I have used programs like sony vegas, premier pro, after effects windows moviemaker, iMovie, and daVinci resolve to make my videos. My love for video editing began when I watched different minecraft youtubers when I was little. I liked how they used editing to compliment the great content they already had. In a way video editing is like html and css, where the html is the content, and the css is what makes the content appealing.
